Welcome to Ocean Invaders Website!

On this website, we will be showing Ocean Invaders and all the little things that makes up this amazing game!

Game Overview:

This game is based in the ocean and the goal of the game is to collect as many fish as possible. Each level will be composed of different difficulty, or increased amount of points, the game getting more challenging as the levels go up. Each level will have different types of fish that give different amounts of points.

*This will be explained in further detail on the rules page!

Inspiration around Ocean Invaders:

When I was a child, my father and I went to a family friend's house and they had a game called Space Invaders. My dad found a lot of joy playing this game and I could see that the game had brought many memories that he is very fond of and brought great joy in his life. Because of this, I have decided to create a game that has taken inspiration from the game, but has many twists and differences. I will further explain this in the rules page listed above.
